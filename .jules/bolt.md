## 2024-05-18 - [Optimization of `Space` Solver in Landscape Evolution Model]
**Learning:** The default numerical solver (`"basic"`) in Landlab's `Space` component utilizes `scipy.integrate.quad` to solve for erosion/deposition equations over each timestep. For landscape evolution models over large grids and extensive timesteps, this creates a major computational bottleneck, significantly extending simulation wall times.
**Action:** Always verify if a specialized, optimized numerical method is available. For `Space` (and similar components computing differential equations over grid cells), use `solver="adaptive"` to bypass generic SciPy quadratures in favor of computationally efficient, domain-specific explicit approximations, resulting in massive performance improvements.
